Regional Adaptations & Localization Changes

Germany

  • Historical imagery like swastikas or Hitler references are censored due to laws against Nazi symbolism.
  • Wolfenstein II: The New Colossus replaced swastikas with triangle symbols and altered Hitler's mustache.

    • China

  • Elements like skeletons, skulls and anything precieved as superstitious practice has been banned
  • WoW redesigned undead characters to cover exposed bones
  • Games that reference Taiwan, Tibet, or Hong Kong related protests and events are likely banned/modified
  • Devotion, a horror game was pulled from the steam marketplace due to hidden in-game text mocking the president of China

    • Saudi Arabia

  • Depictions of LGBTQ+ content, pork, or alcohol are often removed.
  • The Last of Us Part II faced limited distribution in Saudi Arabia and UAE due to a lesbian storyline.
  • God of War (2005) was banned for nudity and mythological references.
  • GTA V is not officially available, due to violence, drugs, and sexual themes.

    • Violence and Gore Moderation

    Japan

  • The Callisto Protocol was canceled in Japan because developers refused to alter gore for a CERO rating.
  • Resident Evil 7 toned down face mutilation and body horror scenes in its Japanese releases, even in the Z Version.
  • Until Dawn censored several violent scenes to avoid tabloid criticism or parental backlash.
  • Resident Evil Village launched worldwide, but with Japanese versions preemptively edited for blood and violence.

    • Australia

  • Left 4 Dead 2: Initially censored due to graphic violence; later released with toned-down content.
  • Manhunt 2: Received an "RC" rating, leading to significant content revisions to achieve an "MA15+" rating.
  • Silent Hill 2025 was Initially banned in Australia due to its extreme content, including graphic violence, child abuse, and drug-induced hallucinations.
  • Super Blood Hockey was benned due to Drug use related to incentives and rewards.
